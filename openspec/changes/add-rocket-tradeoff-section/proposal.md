## Why

The landing page needs a memorable conversion moment that explains Depth Studio's core promise: clients are usually told to choose only two between fast, affordable, and reliable, while Depth Studio's process is designed to deliver all three.

## What Changes

- Add an interactive rocket tradeoff section to the homepage after the services section and before the process section.
- Let users manually toggle Fast, Affordable, and Reliable to make the "pick all three" idea tangible.
- Unlock a strategy-call CTA only after all three values are selected.
- Open a lightweight contact modal from the unlocked CTA so the interaction can lead directly to conversion.

## Impact

- Adds a new client component under `components/sections`.
- Adds section copy/configuration to shared landing content.
- Updates the homepage composition in `app/page.tsx`.
- Extends `app/globals.css` with scoped rocket section and contact modal styles.
